# ☕ Coffee Sales Performance Dashboard

## Overview

The Coffee Sales Performance Dashboard is an interactive Excel-based business intelligence project designed to analyze coffee sales data and transform raw information into actionable insights.

Using Pivot Tables, Pivot Charts, Slicers, and dashboard design techniques, this project provides a comprehensive view of sales performance, customer behavior, product preferences, and revenue trends.

---

## Project Objectives

- Analyze sales performance across different coffee products.
- Identify top-performing countries and customers.
- Evaluate the impact of customer loyalty programs on revenue.
- Monitor sales trends over time.
- Understand roast type and coffee type preferences.
- Support data-driven business decision-making through visualization.

## Key Insights

- Arabica generated the highest revenue among all coffee types.
- The United States contributed the largest share of total sales.
- Loyalty card holders contributed significantly to overall revenue.
- Sales performance demonstrated a positive trend throughout the analysis period.
